# DevArc Product Vision

## Overview

DevArc is an AI-powered developer coaching platform designed to fix the fragmented experience of interview preparation and career growth. Unlike traditional LeetCode-style sites, DevArc focuses on **analyzing the developer's thinking process**, providing elite mentorship that feels like a real-time coach.

DevArc is the **Developer Career Copilot** — helping engineers move from "solving problems" to "mastering the craft."

---

## 🚀 The Core Differentiator: "Understand Thinking"

LeetCode helps you solve problems. **DevArc helps you understand how you solve them.**

By tracking every hint, attempt, and event during a session, DevArc's AI "Insight Engine" builds a profile of your logical strengths and weaknesses. It doesn't just check if your code passed; it evaluates your resilience, logic, and code quality.

---

## 🏗️ Core Product Modules

### 1. The Elite Workspace (Gamified Learning)
- **High-Performance Editor**: Built with Monaco and ZnO-premium dark aesthetics.
- **Dynamic Scoring**: A gamified point system (100 base) where AI hints cost 20 points, encouraging independent struggle before help.
- **Coaching Insights**: Deep AI analysis of the solve timeline, provided immediately after an accepted submission.

### 2. AI Mock Interview Module
- **Track-Aware Agents**: Specialized interviewers for DSA, System Design, Frontend, and Frameworks.
- **Professional Artifacts**: Automatically generates professional-grade PDF reports summarizing performance across Logic, Code, and Communication.

### 3. Career Coaching Layer
- **Personalized Onboarding**: AI analyzes your resume and goals to create a target persona.
- **Dynamic Roadmaps**: A 3-month bespoke technical plan generated by AI to bridge specific gaps in your resume and experience.

---

## 🎯 Target Users

- **Students & Freshers**: Building a structured foundation for entry-level roles.
- **Working Professionals**: Preparing for senior/staff transitions or domain switches (e.g., Frontend to Backend).
- **Elite Prep**: Engineers targeting FAANG/Product giants who need more than just problem-solving skills.

---

## 🔮 The Long-Term Vision

DevArc aims to be the **AI-powered operating system for developer growth**. We are building a world where a developer's potential is measured by their problem-solving DNA, not just their resume.
